PANTAI TLANGOH

Nikmati pesona alam yang unik dan memukau di Bukit Kapur Jaddih, destinasi wisata yang terletak di Bangkalan, Madura. Bukit ini menyajikan panorama tebing kapur putih yang menjulang tinggi, membentuk lanskap eksotis seperti di negeri dongeng.

About Tlangoh

Pantai Pasir Putih Tlangoh, also known as Tlangoh Beach, is a beautiful coastal destination located in Temanah Village, Tlangoh, Tanjung Bumi District, Bangkalan Regency, Madura, East Java,

Indonesia. This beach is renowned for its pristine white sand, clear turquoise waters, and tranquil ambiance, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and recreation.
